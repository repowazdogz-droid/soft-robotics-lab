# Age Banding and Guardrails Contract

This contract defines age-appropriate constraints, parent/teacher visibility rules, and safety boundaries for different age bands.

Version: 0.1  
Effective: 2024-12-13  
Status: Active

---

## 1. Purpose

This contract ensures:
- Age-appropriate learning experiences for all learners
- Enhanced safety for minors
- Appropriate autonomy levels per age band
- Required parental/teacher visibility and oversight
- Digital hygiene curriculum integration

---

## 2. Age Band Definitions

### Age Band: 6-9 (Early Elementary)

Age band 6-9 characteristics:
- Limited reading comprehension
- Concrete thinking predominates
- Requires high scaffolding
- Enhanced safety requirements
- Age band 6-9 has specific constraints

### Age Band: 10-12 (Late Elementary)

Age band 10-12 characteristics:
- Developing abstract thinking
- Increased independence
- Moderate scaffolding appropriate
- Enhanced safety requirements
- Age band 10-12 has specific constraints

### Age Band: 13-15 (Middle School)

Age band 13-15 characteristics:
- Abstract thinking developing
- Increased autonomy possible
- Reduced scaffolding appropriate
- Standard safety requirements
- Age band 13-15 has specific constraints

### Age Band: 16-18 (High School)

Age band 16-18 characteristics:
- Abstract thinking well-developed
- High autonomy possible
- Minimal scaffolding appropriate
- Standard safety requirements
- Age band 16-18 has specific constraints

### Age Band: Adult

Age band adult characteristics:
- Full cognitive development
- Full autonomy allowed
- Minimal scaffolding appropriate
- Standard safety requirements
- Age band adult has specific constraints

## 3. Allowed Tutor Modes Per Age Band

### Age Band 6-9

Allowed modes:
- Explainer mode (primary)
- Coach mode (with high scaffolding)

- Examiner mode (not recommended)

Mode restrictions:
- Complex Socratic questioning is restricted
- Examiner mode requires explicit approval
- Mode restrictions are mandatory
- Violating mode restrictions is prohibited

### Age Band 10-12

Allowed modes:
- Coach mode (primary)
- Explainer mode
- Socratic mode (moderate complexity)
- Examiner mode (with approval)

Mode restrictions:
- Advanced Socratic questioning is restricted
- Examiner mode requires approval
- Mode restrictions are mandatory
- Violating mode restrictions is prohibited

### Age Band 13-15

Allowed modes:
- Socratic mode (primary)
- Coach mode
- Explainer mode
- Examiner mode

Mode restrictions:
- All modes allowed with appropriate scaffolding
- No mode restrictions beyond standard safety
- Mode availability is standard
- No special restrictions required

### Age Band 16-18

Allowed modes:
- Socratic mode (primary)
- Coach mode
- Explainer mode
- Examiner mode

Mode restrictions:
- All modes allowed
- No mode restrictions
- Full mode availability
- No restrictions required

### Age Band Adult

Allowed modes:
- All modes available
- No restrictions
- Full autonomy in mode selection
- User preference determines mode

## 4. Autonomy Levels Per Age Band

### Age Band 6-9: Very Low Autonomy

Autonomy constraints:
- All sessions require explicit adult initiation or approval
- No autonomous session scheduling
- All topics require pre-approval
- Very low autonomy is mandatory
- Exceeding autonomy is prohibited

### Age Band 10-12: Low Autonomy

Autonomy constraints:
- Sessions require adult awareness
- Limited autonomous topic selection
- Sensitive topics require approval
- Low autonomy is mandatory
- Exceeding autonomy is prohibited

### Age Band 13-15: Moderate Autonomy

Autonomy constraints:
- Sessions can be initiated by learner
- Most topics allowed with standard safety
- Sensitive topics require notification
- Moderate autonomy is allowed
- Autonomy within safety boundaries

### Age Band 16-18: High Autonomy

Autonomy constraints:
- Sessions can be fully learner-initiated
- Most topics allowed
- Sensitive topics require acknowledgment
- High autonomy is allowed
- Autonomy within safety boundaries

### Age Band Adult: Full Autonomy

Autonomy constraints:
- Full learner control
- All topics allowed (within platform scope)
- No special restrictions
- Full autonomy is allowed
- User controls all decisions

## 5. Topics Requiring Escalation

### Age Band 6-9: Extensive Escalation

Topics requiring escalation:
- Any topic beyond basic curriculum
- Health and safety topics
- Social and emotional topics
- Complex scientific concepts
- Historical or political topics

Escalation requirements:
- Parent/teacher approval required
- Escalation is mandatory
- Proceeding without approval is prohibited

### Age Band 10-12: Moderate Escalation

Topics requiring escalation:
- Sensitive health topics
- Complex social issues
- Advanced scientific concepts
- Controversial historical topics

Escalation requirements:
- Parent/teacher notification required
- Approval recommended for sensitive topics
- Escalation is mandatory
- Proceeding without notification is prohibited

### Age Band 13-15: Limited Escalation

Topics requiring escalation:
- Highly sensitive topics
- Topics requiring specialized expertise
- Topics with significant safety implications

Escalation requirements:
- Notification recommended
- Approval for highly sensitive topics
- Escalation is recommended
- Proceeding with notification is allowed

### Age Band 16-18: Minimal Escalation

Topics requiring escalation:
- Extremely sensitive topics
- Topics requiring specialized medical/legal expertise

Escalation requirements:
- Notification optional
- Approval for extremely sensitive topics only
- Minimal escalation required
- Most topics proceed without escalation

### Age Band Adult: No Escalation

Topics requiring escalation:
- None (within platform scope)

Escalation requirements:
- No escalation required
- Full user autonomy
- No restrictions

## 6. Required Parental/Teacher Framing

### Age Band 6-9: Full Visibility

Visibility requirements:
- All sessions visible to parents/teachers
- Real-time session monitoring available
- Session summaries provided automatically
- Full visibility is mandatory
- Hiding sessions from adults is prohibited

Framing requirements:
- Parents/teachers receive session summaries
- Learning goals are shared with adults
- Progress updates are provided regularly
- Framing requirements are mandatory

### Age Band 10-12: High Visibility

Visibility requirements:
- All sessions visible to parents/teachers
- Session summaries provided automatically
- Detailed monitoring available on request
- High visibility is mandatory
- Hiding sessions from adults is prohibited

Framing requirements:
- Parents/teachers receive session summaries
- Learning goals are shared with adults
- Progress updates are provided regularly
- Framing requirements are mandatory

### Age Band 13-15: Moderate Visibility

Visibility requirements:
- Session summaries provided to parents/teachers
- Detailed monitoring available on request
- Sensitive topic sessions flagged for review
- Moderate visibility is required
- Complete hiding is prohibited

Framing requirements:
- Parents/teachers receive summaries on request
- Sensitive topics trigger automatic notification
- Progress updates available on request
- Framing requirements are standard

### Age Band 16-18: Limited Visibility

Visibility requirements:
- Session summaries available to parents/teachers on request
- Sensitive topic sessions may trigger notification
- Learner privacy is respected
- Limited visibility is standard
- Complete visibility is optional

Framing requirements:
- Parents/teachers can request summaries
- Sensitive topics may trigger notification
- Progress updates available on request
- Framing requirements are optional

### Age Band Adult: No Visibility Requirement

Visibility requirements:
- No visibility requirements
- Full privacy
- No automatic sharing
- User controls all visibility

- Basic verification ("How do we know?")

Integration:
- Digital hygiene integrated into all sessions
- Concepts introduced naturally
- Basic digital hygiene is mandatory
- Missing digital hygiene is prohibited

### Age Band 10-12: Developing Digital Hygiene

Required topics:
- Source evaluation basics
- Fact vs. opinion distinction
- When information is uncertain
- How to verify claims
- When to be skeptical

Integration:
- Digital hygiene explicitly taught
- Concepts reinforced regularly
- Developing digital hygiene is mandatory
- Missing digital hygiene is prohibited

### Age Band 13-15: Advanced Digital Hygiene

Required topics:
- Critical source evaluation
- Evidence quality assessment
- Uncertainty handling
- Bias recognition
- Verification strategies

Integration:
- Digital hygiene is core curriculum
- Concepts applied in all learning
- Advanced digital hygiene is mandatory
- Missing digital hygiene is prohibited

### Age Band 16-18: Expert Digital Hygiene

Required topics:
- Advanced critical thinking
- Complex source evaluation
- Sophisticated uncertainty handling
- Bias and manipulation recognition
- Independent verification

Integration:
- Digital hygiene is expected competency
- Concepts applied independently
- Expert digital hygiene is expected
- Missing digital hygiene indicates gap

### Age Band Adult: Digital Hygiene Maintenance

Required topics:
- Ongoing critical thinking
- Continuous source evaluation
- Sophisticated uncertainty handling
- Bias and manipulation recognition
- Independent verification

Integration:
- Digital hygiene is maintained
- Concepts applied independently
- Digital hygiene maintenance is expected
- No special requirements

## 9. Age Band Schema

### Age Band Definition Schema

```yaml
AgeBand:
  band_id: string (required, enum: ["6-9", "10-12", "13-15", "16-18", "adult"])
  label: string (required)
  age_min: int (required)
  age_max: int | null (required, null for adult)
  
  allowed_modes: [string] (required, enum: ["Socratic", "Coach", "Examiner", "Explainer"])
  autonomy_level: string (required, enum: ["very_low", "low", "moderate", "high", "full"])
  
  escalation_required_topics: [string] (required)
  visibility_requirements:
    parent_visibility: string (required, enum: ["full", "high", "moderate", "limited", "none"])
    teacher_visibility: string (required, enum: ["full", "high", "moderate", "limited", "none"])
    real_time_monitoring: bool (required)
  
  digital_hygiene_curriculum:
    required_topics: [string] (required)
    integration_level: string (required, enum: ["basic", "developing", "advanced", "expert", "maintenance"])
  
  safety_level: string (required, enum: ["maximum", "high", "standard"])
```

## 10. Age Band Validation

### Age Band Assignment

Age band assignment:
- Age band is assigned based on learner age
- Age band cannot be manually overridden for minors
- Age band assignment is mandatory
- Manual override for minors is prohibited

### Age Band Verification

Age band verification:
- Age band is verified at session start
- Age band mismatch triggers refusal
- Verification is mandatory
- Proceeding with mismatch is prohibited

### Age Band Transitions

Age band transitions:
- Transitions occur automatically on birthday
- Previous sessions remain in original age band context
- Transitions are automatic
- Manual transition is not allowed

## 12. Enforcement

Violations of this contract include:
- Allowing modes not permitted for age band
- Exceeding autonomy levels for age band
- Proceeding with topics requiring escalation without approval
- Hiding sessions from required adults
- Missing digital hygiene curriculum
- Reducing safety below age band requirements

Violations must be:
- Logged as incidents
- Reviewed by system owner
- Corrected immediately
- Documented in change logs

---

## 13. Amendments

Changes to age band definitions, autonomy levels, escalation requirements, or safety boundaries require:
- Impact assessment on learner safety and age-appropriateness
- Testing with representative age band scenarios
- Approval from system owner
- Version increment
- Documentation in change logs
