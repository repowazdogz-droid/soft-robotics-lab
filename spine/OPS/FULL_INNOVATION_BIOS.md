### Innovation Thesis

**Core Innovation:** Turning AI capability into safe, owned outcomes under pressure.

Warren Smith's consulting practice focuses on a specific gap: teams can build AI systems, but they struggle to clearly own the decisions those systems require. The innovation is not in building AI—it's in making decision structure visible, explicit, and defensible before systems scale.

### The Problem It Solves

**The Core Tension:**
- AI systems create capability faster than decision capacity
- Teams ship quickly but can't name who can pause when things go wrong
- Pressure forces implicit trade-offs that become invisible
- Responsibility diffuses across roles and time horizons
- Safety is treated as a feeling, not a mechanism

**Who Experiences This:**
- Engineering teams shipping agent workflows
- Policy teams making high-stakes choices
- Research programmes under delivery pressure
- Safety-critical systems entering real-world operations
- Leaders who need defensible narratives for regulators/boards

### The Innovation

**Decision Inspection Protocol:**
A structured method that makes visible:
- What must be assumed for claims to hold
- Which decisions have no named owner
- Where responsibility diffuses under pressure
- What would trigger a pause or escalation
- Which trade-offs are being made implicitly

**Not:**
- Forecasting or prediction
- Optimization or automation
- Therapy or mediation
- A replacement for judgement

**Is:**
- A way to slow down the right moments
- A shared reference for difficult conversations
- A tool for inspecting decisions, not automating them
- A briefing aid for leaders and teams

### The Method

**Typical Engagement Flow:**
1. **30–45 min triage call** — Quick assessment of fit
2. **1–2 week review** — Deep inspection of decision structure
3. **Short memo + responsibility map** — Actionable deliverables

**What Gets Produced:**
- A short decision memo (what's being decided, by whom, by when)
- A responsibility + stop map (who owns what, who can pause, escalation paths)
- A ship / pause / redesign call (explicit recommendation with reasoning)
- Reusable language for the room (shared vocabulary for ongoing decisions)

### Innovation Characteristics

**1. Clarity Over Certainty**

**2. Ownership Over Optimization**

**3. Structure Over Strategy**

- Focuses on "what must be decided" not "what should we do"

**4. Inspection Over Advice**

**Omega-C (Control):**
