# Trauma-Informed Education System

## V1 Lock Statement

**This system is complete at V1.**

V1 is a complete, usable system. It is not provisional, incomplete, or in development. Schools can use it immediately and independently. No waiting for completion or updates.

---

## What This System Is

### A Complete Operating System

**This system is:**
- A complete, self-contained operating system for trauma-informed practice in schools
- A framework of principles, processes, and tools that schools adapt to their context
- Evidence-informed, drawing on principles from trauma-informed education research
- Designed to work independently, without requiring founder presence or ongoing support
- Focused on creating supportive environments and system changes, not individual interventions

**This system provides:**
- Foundational principles and language definitions
- Core framework (pillars, role translations, process maps)
- Delivery modules (8 self-paced modules)
- Implementation cycles and reflection tools
- AI guardrails and prompt schemas (if AI is available)
- Governance and safeguarding boundaries
- Evidence and claims discipline guidance

---

## What This System Is NOT

### Explicit Non-Functions

**This system is NOT:**
- Therapy delivery or therapeutic interventions
- Clinical assessment, diagnosis, or treatment
- Case consultation or individual child advice
- Crisis intervention or emergency response
- An evidence-based intervention with research-proven outcomes
- A programme requiring compliance or fidelity
- Ongoing consultancy or service delivery (by default)
- A training course requiring trainers or schedules

**This system does NOT:**
- Promise or guarantee specific outcomes
- Provide clinical or therapeutic support
- Assess or diagnose children
- Offer case consultation or advice
- Create dependency on founder or system creator
- Require constant updates or changes
